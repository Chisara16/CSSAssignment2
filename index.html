Difference between Paddings, Margins and Borders

Padding is the spacing that occurs between the border box and the content box. It is applied to created gap in between a border line and the content. The content here could be a text, image, logo, symbol etc.

Margin is the spacing between the border/content and the main page.

Borders are enclosed box-like feautures we use to hold,categorize or group contents together.
